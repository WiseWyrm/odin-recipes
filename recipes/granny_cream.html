Granny Cream's Hot Butter Ice Cream

Very Tasty And Healty Too!

An old time favorite for those cold winter night's

Ingredients

  • 1-3 sticks of butter
  • Ice cream of your choice

how to make

  1. Take the sticks of butter and put them in a microwave safe container and heat them in microwave until melted or until some clumps remain for texture
  2. Scoop ice cream into bowl
  3. Pour hot butter into ice cream and mix
  4. Serve up, and enjoy!
